Admission Requirements

FOR GRADUATING SHS STUDENTS

  1. Certified True Copy of Grade 11 SHS Final Report Card
  2. CMU Recommendation Form accomplished and signed by the Class Adviser, School Principal, and Guidance Counselor
  3. Certificate of Good Moral Character signed by the Guidance Counselor/ Prefect of Discipline and School Principal
  4. Applicant’s or Parent’s/ Guardian/s Certificate of Voter’s Registration from COMELEC Malabon
  5. Barangay certificate of Indigency
  6. 1pc. 2x2 picture with white background

FOR GRADUATES OF PREVIOUS SCHOOL YEARS

  1. Certified True Copy of Grade 11 SHS Final Report Card
  2. CMU Recommendation Form accomplished and signed by the Class Adviser, School Principal, and Guidance Counselor
  3. Certificate of Good Moral Character signed by the Guidance Counselor/ Prefect of Discipline and School Principal
  4. Applicant’s or Parent’s/ Guardian/s Certificate of Voter’s Registration from COMELEC Malabon
  5. Barangay certificate of Indigency
  6. 1pc. 2x2 picture with white background

PASSERS OF ALS

  1. Certified True Copy of ALS Report Card with 100% percentile rating or better
  2. Certificate of Good Moral Character signed by the Guidance Counselor/ Prefect of Discipline and School Principal
  3. Applicant’s or Parent’s/ Guardian/s Certificate of Voter’s Registration from COMELEC Malabon
  4. Barangay certificate of Indigency
  5. 1pc. 2x2 picture with white background

FOR TRANSFEREES/RETURNEES

  1. Photocopy (CTC) of Transcript of Record
  2. Certificate of Good Moral Character signed by the Guidance Counselor/ Prefect of Discipline and School Principal
  3. Applicant’s or Parent’s/ Guardian/s Certificate of Voter’s Registration from COMELEC Malabon
  4. Barangay certificate of Indigency
  5. 1pc. 2x2 picture with white background (Computer generated pictures are not allowed)

EXEMPTION FROM UNDERGRAD ADMISSION TEST AND ADMISSION FEE

SHS Graduating Honor Students (With Highest Honors) and Student-Athletes are eligible to apply for EXEMPTION from taking the Undergrad Admission Test and paying the admission fee. Such exemptions may be granted upon submission of the following documents:

  1. Certified True Copy of Grade 11 SHS Final Report Card
  2. CMU Recommendation Form accomplished and signed by the Class Adviser, School Principal, and Guidance Counselor
  3. Certificate of Good Moral Character signed by the Guidance Counselor/ Prefect of Discipline and School Principal
  4. Applicant’s or Parent’s/ Guardian/s Certificate of Voter’s Registration from COMELEC Malabon
  5. Barangay certificate of Indigency
  6. 1pc. 2x2 picture with white background
  7. Certification from the School Principal
  • For Honor Students: With Highest Honors
  • For Student-Athletes: Participation in National and International Sports Competitions

Application Process

  1. Accomplishment of the Online Admission Application Form
  2. Pay the admission fee at the Finance Office
  3. Take the CMU Undergraduate Admission Examination
  4. Pass the Academic Interview
  5. Receive the Acceptance Notice
  6. Sign the scholarship contract and receive the scholarship certificate
  7. Enroll onsite
