Admission Requirements

  1. FOR PCCAT EXAMINEE INITIAL REQUIREMENTS

    • Duly accomplished PCCAT Application Form (w/ 2x2 picture)
    • Form 138 of Grade 11 and 12 (Photocopy)
    • Voter’s certificate from COMELEC (must be a registered resident and voter of Paranaque City)

  2. FOR ENROLLMENT

    • Duly accomplished admission form
    • PSA birth certificate/ marriage certificate if married (original)
    • Voter’s certificate from COMELEC (must be a registered resident and voter of Paranaque City, photocopy)
    • certificate
    • Diploma
    • Form 138 (original)
    • 3 pcs. of 2x2 picture (with white background and printed name)
    • 2 pcs. of long brown and plastic envelope

  3. FOR PASSERS OF ALS/PEPT

    • 2pcs. of long brown and plastic envelope
    • Unique learner identifier (ULI number)
    • To get your ULI number visit: https://t2mis.tesda.gov.ph/Barangay
