Admission Requirements

FOR GRADUATING SHS STUDENTS

  1. High School card (Form 138)
  2. Certification of good moral character duly signed by School Principal or Registrar
  3. Result of College Admission Test
  4. Authenticated Birth Certificate

FOR TRANSFEREES

  1. Certification of Honorable Dismissal
  2. True copies of grades, Transcript of Record from school last attended
  3. Result of College Admission Test
  4. Certificate of Good Moral Character

FOR OLD STUDENTS

  1. Curriculum checklist properly filled-out with grades in all the subjects taken

BACHELOR PROGRAMS

Applicants to any of the curriculum programs of the institute must:

  1. be a High School graduate
  2. have passed the College Admission Test
  3. have passed the College Personal Interview
  4. have an average grade of at least 85% in English and Math (for CAS and CED)
  5. be physically and mentally fit, as duly certified by the School Physician

GRADUATE PROGRAMS

  1. Transcript of Records
  2. Qualifying Exam
  3. Permit to study, if employed in the government
  4. Certificate of employment indicating work schedule, if employed in a private company
  5. Transfer credentials and/or TOR with special order, if graduate

QUALIFICATIONS FOR SHORT-TERM COURSES

  1. Can read and write
  2. High School graduate with knowledge in basic computer (computer programs)

Application Process

  1. Online register in the EARIST Pinnacle
  2. After completing the registration online, Sign in/Login to your account and look for the application form.
  3. Click the Personal Data Form and Print it in A4 Bond Paper and bring the Original/Certified True copy of Documents.
  4. Bring the Original Required Documents and Printable Online Admission Form/Personal Data form with Applicant Number to the Registrar's Office for Evaluation/Verification and to Secure ECAT Examination Permit.
