Admission Requirements

DOCUMENTARY REQUIREMENTS

For Examination:
  1. Apply online thru Google Form
  2. Need to fill out the QCU Admission Application Form
FOR FRESHMEN
  1. Printed (back to back on one single sheet of paper) - Fully Accomplished QCU Admission Application Form with 2x2 colored picture white background with name tag and student’s signature at the back on the space below the form. (2 sets)
  2. Original SF9 (Certified True Copy of Grade 11 & Original Grade 12) Form 138- High School Report Card | Alternative Learning System Certificate of Rating if ALS passer
  3. Original PSA Birth Certificate
  4. Original Certificate of Good Moral Character
  5. Original – Recent Brgy. Certificate of Residency
  6. Two(2) 2x2 colored pictures (White Background with Name Tag)
  7. Photocopy Senior High School / High School / ALS Diploma (present original)
  8. Medical Clearance – Issued by the Medical and Dental Services
  9. Additional Requirements for the following Applicants:
    • If with discrepancy between PSA Birth Certificate and School Record – Affidavit of the Same Person
    • If currently employed - Original Latest Certificate of Employment
    • If Married Female Student - Original Marriage Certificate
    • Applicants from schools with non-numerical (Pass/Fail) grades- Certificate of Non-issuance of Numerical Grades (Issued by the School Registrar)
  10. In cases where the student-applicant cannot reasonably secure his/her school credentials for reasons beyond his/her control, such as: Closure of the school last attended and Damage of documents due to fire or natural calamities - Certificate of eligibility for admission to college from the Commission on Higher Education (CHED)
  11. Applicants who stopped for 1 Yr or more - Affidavit of Non-Enrollment from other Universities
FOR TRANSFEREES
  1. Printed (back to back on one single sheet of paper) Fully Accomplished QCU Admission Application Form with 2x2 colored picture white background with name tag and student’s signature at the back on the space below the form. (2 sets)
  2. Transcript of Records (For Evaluation Purposes) with General Weighted Average (GWA) of the last semester attended.
  3. Original PSA Birth Certificate
  4. Original Certificate of Good Moral Character
  5. Original – Recent Brgy. Certificate of Residency
  6. Two (2) 2x2 pictures (White Background with Name Tag).
  7. Original Certificate of Transfer Credentials (CTC)
  8. Medical Clearance – Issued by the Medical and Dental Services
  9. Additional Requirements for the following Applicants:
    • For Accreditation of Subjects during enrollment period – Subject Course Description of all passed subjects, TOR Copy for QCU and NSTP 2 Serial Number (Secure request letter from QCU Admissions Office)
  10. If with discrepancy between PSA Birth Certificate and School Record – Affidavit of the Same Person
  11. If currently employed - Original Latest Certificate of Employment
  12. If Married Female Student - Original Marriage Certificate
  13. Applicants from schools with non-numerical (Pass/Fail) grades- Certificate of Non-issuance of Numerical Grades (Issued by the School Registrar)
  14. In cases where the student-applicant cannot reasonably secure his/her school credentials for reasons beyond his/her control, such as: Closure of the school last attended and Damage of documents due to fire or natural calamities - Certificate of eligibility for admission to college from the Commission on Higher Education (CHED)
  15. Applicants who stopped for 1 Yr. or more - Affidavit of Non-Enrollment from other Universities

Application Process

Freshmen and Transfer Applicants

(Second-year or Third-year students only) should follow these steps:

  1. Create Gmail account with this format: lastname.firstname.middlename@gmail.com or lastname.firstname.middlename1@gmail.com

    NOTE: yahoo mail and deped.qc emails are not accepted. Follow email format for easy identification of the applicant.

  2. Download and Fill out QCU Student Application form: https://drive.google.com/…/1A9ntILxa5lSOOmAFVMT…/view…

    Register and Upload Form here: https://tinyurl.com/QCUCAT-2024-1ST-SEM2
