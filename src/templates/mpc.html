Admission Requirements

FOR GRADUATING SHS STUDENTS

  1. Photocopy of latest report card (Form 138)
  2. Original and photocopy of certificate of good moral character
  3. Three (3) pieces 2x2 ID picture with name tag and white background

FOR TRANSFEREES

  1. Photocopy of Transcript of Records/ Certificate of Grades
  2. Original and photocopy of certificate of good moral character
  3. Photocopy of honorable dismissal certificate or transfer credentials
  4. Three (3) pieces 2x2 ID Picture with Name Tag and White Background

FOR PASSERS OF ALS/PEPT

  1. Certificate of Rating (COR)
  2. Three (3) pieces 2x2 ID Picture with Name Tag and White Background

FOR RETURNEES

  1. Please proceed directly to the registrar’s office
  2. Bring scanned copy of birth certificat
