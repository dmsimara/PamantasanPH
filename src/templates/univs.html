Polytechnic University of the Philippines

1016 Anonas, Sta. Mesa, Maynila, Kalakhang Maynila

List of Requirements

  1. Passed PUP College Entrance Exam Test (PUPCET)
  2. Confirmation Requirements
  3. High School Graduate
  4. High School Card (Form 138)
  5. Form 137-A
  6. Medical Clearance from PUP Medical Clinic
  7. Certification of Good Moral Character duly signed by the high school principal/guidance counselor of the school last attended.
  8. NSO authenticated copy of Birth Certificate/ Certificate of Live Birth

University Specialization

  • Education
  • Information Technology
  • Management
  • Marketing

Steps to Follow

  1. Take the PUP College Entrance Exam Test (PUPCET)
  2. Wait for the results of the exam
  3. Submit the required documents
  4. Pay the necessary fees
  5. Attend the orientation

Programs Offered

  • Bachelor of Science in Business Administration
  • Bachelor of Science in Computer Science
  • Bachelor of Science in Electronics and Communications Engineering
  • Bachelor of Science in Elementary Education
  • Bachelor of Science in Secondary Education

  • Quezon City
