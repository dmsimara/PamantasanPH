Admission Requirements

  • UNDERGRADUATE
    • INCOMING FIRST YEAR STUDENTS
      • Admissions Profile Form
      • Form 138-A with original signature of the School Principal
      • Certificate of Good Moral Character
      • PSA-authenticated Birth Certificate
      • Certified Photocopy of Marriage Contract (for married women only)
      • PSA-authenticated Birth Certificate of Husband (for married women only)
      • 5pcs. recent 2×2 ID picture
      • 1 short brown envelope
      • 1 long brown envelope
      • 1 window envelope with mailing stamp
    • RETURNING STUDENTS
      • Admissions Profile Form
      • University Clearance
      • Report of Grades (ROG) for the last semester attended
      • 1 window envelope with mailing stamp
    • TRANSFEREES
      • Admissions Profile Form
      • Informative Copy of the Transcript of Records (TOR)
      • Certificate of Transfer Credential or Honorable Dismissal
      • Certificate of Good Moral Character
      • PSA-authenticated Birth Certificate
      • 5pcs. recent 2×2 ID picture
      • 1 short brown envelope
      • 1 long brown envelope
      • 1 window envelope with mailing stamp
  • GRADUATE SCHOOL
    • MSES
      • Bachelor’s Degree
      • Good moral / Academic record
      • Honorable Dismissal for non-DOCST graduates and transferees
      • Completed application & Admission forms
      • Photocopy of Transcript of Records (TOR)
      • Two (2) Recommendation Letter as reference (academic reference and employment reference)
      • NSO copy of birth certificate
      • Medical certificate
      • Two (2) copies of recent 2×2 ID picture
      • Two (2) copies of recent 1×1 ID picture
  • UNDERGRADUATE SCHOLARSHIPS
    • College Entrance Examination for Admission and Scholarship (CEEAS)
      • Original copy of Form 138 with an average of 90% and above (Senior High School Report Card)
      • Certificate of Good Moral Character
      • Photocopy of the student's Birth Certificate
      • Photocopy of the student's Birth Certificate of Parents/Guardians
      • 2 copies of latest 2x2 ID picture
      • Recommendation from the school Principal or Guidance Counselor

Application Process

The admission process for Davao Oriental State University involves the following steps:

  • Complete the Admission Profile Form available at the Admissions Office or the university website.
  • Submit all the required documents.
  • Attend the scheduled interview and examination.
  • Wait for the release of the admission results.
