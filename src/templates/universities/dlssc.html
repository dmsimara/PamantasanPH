NOT YET OPENED

Admission Requirements

  • ADMISSION CREDENTIALS
    • INCOMING FIRST-YEAR STUDENT
      • Senior High School Report Card (Form 138) or its equivalent from the school last attended with the eligibility certificate indicated and signed by the authorized school official
      • Certificate of Good Moral Character from the last school attended
      • Birth Certificate authenticated by PSA (photocopy)
      • DSSC Admission Test result
      • Four (4) pieces of passport size picture with white background and name tag
      • Three (3) copies of 2x2 colored ID picture
      • Physical examination and medical clearance from DSSC medical doctor
    • RETURNEE STUDENT
      • Evaluation of grades signed by the respective Dean
      • Four (4) copies of 2x2 colored ID picture
      • Honorable dismissal/Transfer Credentials
      • Medical Clearance from DSSC medical doctor
    • TRANSFER STUDENT
      • Evaluation of grades signed by the respective Dean
      • Four (4) pieces of passport size picture with white background and name tag
      • Three (3) copies of 2x2 colored ID picture
      • Medical Clearance from DSSC medical doctor
    • GRADUATE STUDENT
      • Original or authenticated copy of Transcript of Records (TOR)
      • Four (4) pieces of passport size picture with white background and name tag
      • Three (3) copies of 2x2 colored ID picture
      • Medical Clearance from DSSC medical doctor

General Requirements

  • Original and Photocopy of Senior High School Card
  • Original and Photocopy of NSO/PSA Birth Certificate
  • Certificate of Good Moral Character
  • 4 pcs. of 2x2 recent colored photos
  • 2 pcs. of 1x1 ID photo
  • Physical Exam Results
  • Accomplished Application Form
