Application Process

  • I. ADMISSION PROCEDURE
    • Submit the requirements at the Registrar’s Office
    • Secure a copy of your School Identification Number (SIN)
    • Proceed to the Dean’s Office for evaluation of your credentials
    • Present your SIN to the Dean for admission and to be included in the list of examinees
    • Pay the application fee at the cashier and secure an official receipt
    • Submit the accomplished application form and official receipt at the Dean’s Office
    • Secure your permit to take the entrance examination at the Registrar’s Office
  • II. ONLINE APPLICATION PROCEDURE
    • Sign-up and fill out the application form
    • Upload the necessary documents in PDF format (except for ID picture)
    • Check your application status and schedule for the online examination
    • Take the online entrance examination
    • Wait for the results and further instructions through email
