Admission Requirements

Qualifications

  1. Applicant must be a resident of Taguig City (including “EMBO” barangays) for at least three (3) years before the application
  2. With at least one parent a registered voter of the City (or EMBO)
  3. If the applicant is 18 years old or older, the applicant must be a registered voter of the City (including EMBO)
  4. Currently enrolled or graduate of public or private senior high school (SHS) in Taguig (or EMBO) or nearby and contiguous LGU in Metro Manila.

DOCUMENTARY REQUIREMENTS

Prepare TWO (2) SETS in a long brown envelope

  1. SET 1 (all photocopies) – to be submitted to TCU Admission Office
  2. SET 2 (original copies) – for verification
    • Grade 11 Report Card duly signed by the School Principal
    • Grade 12 Report Card (1st semester only for graduating students) duly signed by the School Principal
    • Certificate of Good Moral Character issued after February 2024
    • Recent two (2) pieces 2x2 picture in collared shirt with white background and name tag (Ex. Dela Crus, Juan Tan)
    • Birth certificate (issued by PSA)
    • Voter’s certification issued by the COMELEC showing that one of the parents of the applicant is a registered voter of the city. Voter’s Certification of the applicant if eighteen (18) years old or olders. (Certification must be issued after May 2022)
    • ALS Certificate (if ALS Graduate)
    • Death Certificate (parents, if claimed to be deceased, issued by PSA)
    • Work contract/Visa/ Passport (if any or both parents are OFWs)
    • COMELEC Stub issued after May 2022 Elections (for applicants/ or parents who are newly registered or those who have applied for reactivation)

Application Process

  1. Enter at TCU Gate 2 (in front of UBNHS). Show the screenshot of your Proof of Application to the Guard on duty
  2. Go to the TCU Basement of the Academic Building. Check and verify your name in the masterlist ONLINE or its printed copy at the lobby. If you are NOT in the list, it means we did not receive your online application, and you will NOT be entertained. Then proceed to the Left Wing Auditorium to fill out the TCU Admission Application Checklist.
  3. Proceed to the University Auditorium and present your original documents and its photocopies to the validator for evaluation and validation.
  4. Once the documents have been validated and received by teh validators TEST PERMIT will be issued to you by the MIS Personnel.
