Navotas Polytechnic College

Kapitbahayan Multi-Purpose Hall, North Bay Boulevard South Kaunlaran, Navotas City

NOT YET OPENED

Admission Requirements

FOR GRADUATING SHS STUDENTS

  1. Senior High School Card
  2. Certificate of Good Moral Character with dry seal
  3. Student’s COMELEC Navotas Voter’s ID/Certification/Registration stub (Parents’ COMELEC Navotas Voter’s ID/Certification/Registration stub are not accepted)
  4. PSA/ NSO authenticated birth certificate
  5. 2 pcs. Passport size photo with white background and name tag

FOR TRANSFEREES

  1. Transcript of Record/ Certificate of Grades with dry seal
  2. Honorable Dismissal with dry seal
  3. Certificate of Good Moral Character with dry seal
  4. Student’s COMELEC Navotas Voter’s ID/Certification/Registration stub (Parents’ COMELEC Navotas Voter’s ID/Certification/Registration stub are not accepted)
  5. PSA/ NSO authenticated birth certificate
  6. 2 pcs. Passport size photo with white background and name tag

FOR PASSERS OF ALS

  1. ALS Certification of Rating/Certification of Passing the Accreditation & Equivalency Test (A & E).
  2. Certificate of Good Moral Character with dry seal
  3. Student’s COMELEC Navotas Voter’s ID/Certification/Registration stub (Parents’ COMELEC Navotas Voter’s ID/Certification/Registration stub are not accepted)
  4. PSA/ NSO authenticated birth certificate
  5. 2 pcs. Passport size photo with white background and name tag
  6. ALS Passers of School Year 2019-2020 onward are not qualified. As per DepEd Order No. 013 series of 2019, they should complete the two-year formal Senior High School program (Grades 11 and 12).

FOR HIGH SCHOOL GRADUATES OF OLD CURRICULUM

  1. High School Card
  2. Certificate of Good Moral Character with dry seal
  3. Certification that the Form 137 has not been sent to any other colleges or universities with dry seal. For the sample copy, visit this link: https://drive.google.com/file/d/1QdAgLmYhyN1j0qrv1mA7U9sDonge85Ff/view?usp=sharing
  4. Student’s COMELEC Navotas Voter’s ID/Certification/Registration stub (Parents’ COMELEC Navotas Voter’s ID/Certification/Registration stub are not accepted)
  5. PSA/ NSO authenticated birth certificate
  6. 2 pcs. Passport size photo with white background and name tag
