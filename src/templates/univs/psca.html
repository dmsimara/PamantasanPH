Admission Requirements

  1. FOR GRADUATING SHS STUDENTS

    Bring the Photocopy of the following:

    • Senior High School Card (Grade 11) and/or Recent Grading Period (Grade 12)
    • Certificate of Good Moral Character
    • 2 pcs 1×1 latest colored picture

  2. FOR TRANSFEREES

    Bring the Photocopy of the following:

    • Transcript of Records
    • Honorable Dismissal
    • Certificate of Good Moral Character
    • 2 pcs 1×1 latest colored picture

  3. OTHER REQUIREMENTS

    • ISHIHARA Exam Results for InET Courses

  4. GRADUATE PROGRAMS

    • For Admission examination, the applicant must submit/comply with the following requirements
    • Certified Photocopy of High School Card of Last Grading Period (for Freshmen)
    • Certified Photocopy of Transcript of Records (for transferees)
    • Certified Photocopy of Certificate of Good Moral Character
    • 1 piece 1×1 Latest Colored Picture
    • P300.00 Admission Test Fee
    • A General Weighted Average of 85% is required for BSAeE (Bachelor of Science in Aeronautical Engineering) and BSAT (Bachelor of Science in Air Transportation) applicants; and without any grade requirement for all other courses.
    • For transferees, A Weighted Average of 80% or above in the latest semester they have attended is required.

Application Process

  1. Accomplish the online application form.
  2. Prepare and scan all required documents.
  3. Submit the accomplished application form and scanned documents online.
  4. Wait for the email notification for the schedule of the admission test and interview.
  5. Take the admission test and interview.
  6. Wait for the admission decision.

Step by Step Application Process

  • Step 1: Submission of Admission Requirements

    Freshmen

    • Clear copy (jpeg/pdf) of the following documents (to be uploaded in the ISMS link):
      • Senior High School card (Grade 11 & 1st Semester of Grade 12)
      • Certificate of Good Moral Character
      • 1×1 Latest colored picture in white background

    Transferees

    • Clear copy (jpeg/pdf) of the following documents (to be uploaded in the ISMS):
      • Transcript of Records (for evaluation purposes subject to existing policy on validation and accreditation of courses)
      • Honorable Dismissal
      • Certificate of Good Moral Character
      • 1×1 Latest colored picture in white background

    Reminder: An official list of qualified applicants for interview will be released. A notice for an interview will be sent to the applicant's email.

  • Step 2: Interview Additional Requirements

    During the interview, the following additional requirements must be submitted (to be uploaded in the ISMS) by applicants in the following programs:

    • BS Air Transportation
    • BS Aeronautical Engineering
    • BS Aircraft Maintenance Technology
    • BS Aviation Electronics Technology
    • BS Aviation Communication major in Flight Operation

    Additional requirements:

    • An Ishihara Test (Negative for Colorblindness) from any DOH-Accredited Clinic or Hospital.
    • Neuro-psychological Test result from any DOH Accredited Clinic or Hospital (with diagnosis) for applicants in the BS Air Transportation Program.

    Reminder: A notice of Acceptance will be sent through e-mail to successful applicants.

  • Step 3: Enrollment
